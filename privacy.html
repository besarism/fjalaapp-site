Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Fjala, we take the privacy and safety of children very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Fjala mobile application. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable privacy laws.

Fjala is designed for children ages 3-8 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verified parental consent.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our educational services:

Information Provided by Parents/Guardians

  • Child's First Name: Optional, used only to personalize the learning experience within the app
  • Parent/Guardian Email: Required for account management and subscription communications
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store (we do not store payment details)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Learning Progress: Which letters have been completed, tracing accuracy, and game scores
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent learning, and session frequency
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to improve app stability

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Child's last name or full name
  • Child's date of birth or exact age
  • Physical address or precise geolocation
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Social security numbers or other government IDs
  • Social media information

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and personalizing the learning experience
  • Tracking learning progress and achievements
  • Improving app features and educational content
  • Processing subscriptions and managing accounts
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending important app updates and subscription notifications
  • Ensuring technical functionality and fixing bugs

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Most learning data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Servers: Any cloud-stored data is protected by enterprise-grade security measures
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to any collected data
  • No Persistent Identifiers: We do not use advertising IDs or tracking cookies

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety and legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notification to users)

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For payment processing and subscription management
  • Cloud Storage: For backing up learning progress (optional feature)

COPPA Compliance

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requires that we inform parents and guardians about our privacy practices for children under 13.

Parental Consent

Before collecting any personal information from a child, we require verified parental consent through:

  • Parent email verification
  • Agreement to terms during account creation
  • Subscription authorization through App Store/Google Play

Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review any personal information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse to allow further collection or use of their child's information
  • Access and download learning progress data

To exercise these rights, contact us at b3sar11@gmail.com

No Advertising or Tracking

Fjala is a safe, ad-free environment for children:

  • No third-party advertising networks
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling
  • No interest-based advertising
  • No social media integration or sharing features
  • No external links without parental gates

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Learning Progress: Retained while subscription is active and for 30 days after cancellation
  • Account Information: Retained until account deletion is requested
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years for quality assurance

After retention periods expire or upon request, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

International Users

Fjala is available worldwide. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

We comply with applicable international privacy laws including GDPR for European users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify users through the app and/or email for material changes
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

Your Choices and Controls

You have control over your child's information:

  • Disable Progress Syncing: Keep all data stored locally on your device
  • Delete Account: Permanently remove all associated data from our servers
  • Cancel Subscription: Stop payment without losing local progress data
  • Contact Support: Request data access, correction, or deletion at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email:

b3sar11@gmail.com

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

For COPPA-related inquiries or to exercise parental rights, please include "COPPA Request" in your email subject line.
